Privacy Policy

GPS Location Changer

GPS Location Changer is a mock GPS and route testing tool designed for developers, testers, learners, and users who need to simulate location behavior through Android's official mock location workflow.

Effective date: June 5, 2026 Package name: com.locationlab

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how GPS Location Changer handles information when you use the app. The app is built to run mock location tests on your device. It does not require an account, does not sell personal information, and does not include advertising or third-party analytics in the current version.

The app works only through Android's official mock location system. You must manually select GPS Location Changer as the mock location app in Android Developer Options before mock location testing can run.

Information the App Uses

The app may use the following information to provide its features:

  • Device location: Used to center the map on your current area when you choose current location or reset location features.
  • Selected mock coordinates: Latitude and longitude values you choose on the map, enter manually, select from search results, save, or use in route testing.
  • Saved locations and folders/tags: Names, folder labels, favorite status, and coordinates you save inside the app.
  • Route test data: Route stops, movement profile, route progress, and speed settings used for route simulation features.
  • Service status logs: Local logs that help show whether mock location testing started, stopped, updated, or failed.
  • App settings: Language, theme, unit preferences, and coordinate format.

How Information Is Stored

Saved locations, route-related test data, service logs, and app settings are stored locally on your device. They are used to make the app work and to help you repeat testing scenarios.

GPS Location Changer does not operate a cloud account system for this data in the current version. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, locally stored app information may be deleted by Android.

Location Permission

GPS Location Changer requests location permission so the app can show your current area on the map and support location testing workflows. Location permission is also related to the foreground service used while mock location testing is active.

The app does not use background location permission in the current version. Mock location testing is controlled by you through the app's Start and Stop controls and Android's mock location provider selection.

Foreground Service and Notifications

When mock location testing is active, the app may run a foreground service and display a persistent notification. This is used to keep the test session visible and controllable while location updates are being sent to Android's mock location system.

The notification helps you understand when mock location testing is active and provides a way to stop the active session.

Google Maps and Places

GPS Location Changer uses Google Maps and Google Places features to display maps, search for addresses or places, and show search suggestions. These services are provided by Google and may process information such as map interactions, search text, device information, and network information according to Google's own policies.

You can review Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Exported Test Reports

The app can generate a test session report that may include selected coordinates, app settings, mock location status, and service events. Reports are created only when you choose to export them.

Once you share, upload, or send an exported report using another app or service, that external app or service may handle the report according to its own privacy policy.

Data Sharing

GPS Location Changer does not sell your personal information. The app does not share your saved locations, route test data, or service logs with an app-operated server in the current version.

Limited information may be processed by third-party services used by the app, such as Google Maps and Google Places, when you use map and search features.

Children's Privacy

GPS Location Changer is intended as a developer, QA, educational, and technical testing utility. It is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can deny location permission, although some app features may not work fully.
  • You can stop mock location testing at any time using the app or notification controls.
  • You can delete saved locations and clear service logs inside the app.
  • You can clear all app data through Android system settings.
  • You can remove GPS Location Changer as the selected mock location app in Developer Options.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
